About Braap

Our mission is to organize mechanical part information and automate the service process, so when something breaks finding the parts and someone to fix it is easy.

Verb

“According to several sources and our own interpretation “Braap” defines a flow, a connection between a customer and technician. We explain it as the unison of working together to maintain, diagnose, and fix mechanical problems in a comprehensive yet simple collaborative environment.”

Our Story

Experience built Braap, along with a lifetime of knowledge. Over the past several decades, our team’s hands-on experience with parts, service, and advanced technology cumulated into a service platform that connects diagnostics to parts and customers to technicians in a highly automated yet flexible environment.

Braap isn’t a couple of guys in a garage but is a vast network of suppliers, technicians, and technology that have a common interest in being the best and delivering an experience that gives simple solutions for fixing things when (or before) they break.

Guided by our mission of making the mechanical things we rely on to be more dependable and safer; Braap is committed to building technology that makes diagnosing, maintaining, and servicing a lot easier.

A comprehensive, simple-to-use turnkey platform for service and repair businesses that offers unique features that enhance customer and technician collaboration.
