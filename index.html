How Braap Works

Mechanical parts break, when things go wrong they need fixed. Long before you need a diagnosis for your problem Braap is connecting the millions of parts, maintenance requirements, and service professionals so you can easily get what you need.

Organizing and Connecting

Braap connects diagnostics, know-how, parts, and service technicians together on one platform to solve problems. Our AI engine helps keep track of maintenance needs, fixes, and everything else to make your mechanical device run its best.

In a fraction of seconds, Braap uses its algorithms and ranking system to sort through millions of potential diagnostics and parts to find the most relevant result for what you are looking for.
